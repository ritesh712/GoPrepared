IIT-JEE

IIT JEE is a national level engineering entrance exam consisting of two stages,
JEE Main & JEE Advanced. JEE Main is a screening exam to select the candidates
who will be eligible to appear for JEE Advanced, which will serve as final exam to
select candidates for admission into prestigious IITs. Every year, the IITs conduct
a Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) to select candidates for the 4-year B.Tech,
B.Pharma, 5-year B.Arch, Integrated M.Sc. and M.Tech courses.
